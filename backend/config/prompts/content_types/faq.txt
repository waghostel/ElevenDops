# Content Type: Disease FAQ

## Goal
Generate a FAQ-style patient education script answering common questions about the disease or condition in an accessible Q&A format.

## Format
Structure the script as a series of natural questions and answers:
- "A common question patients ask is: [question]"
- "The answer is: [clear, simple answer]"

## Required Topics (extract from document)
1. What is this condition?
2. What causes it?
3. What are the symptoms?
4. How is it diagnosed?
5. What are the treatment options?
6. What can I do at home to manage it?
7. When should I see a doctor?

## Tone Guidelines
- Conversational Q&A format
- Address patient worries and common misconceptions
- Empathetic and educational
- Use phrases like "You might be wondering..." and "Many patients ask..."
