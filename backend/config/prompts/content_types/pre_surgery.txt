# Content Type: Pre-Surgery Education

## Goal
Generate a PRE-SURGERY patient education script that helps patients prepare for their upcoming procedure.

## Required Sections
1. **Opening** - Warm welcome and introduction to the procedure
2. **What to Expect** - Brief overview of the surgery/procedure
3. **Pre-operative Preparation** - Fasting requirements, medications to stop/continue
4. **Day of Surgery** - What to bring, arrival time, check-in process
5. **Questions to Ask** - Encourage patient engagement with their care team
6. **Closing** - Reassurance and contact information for concerns

## Tone Guidelines
- Reassuring and calming (patients may be anxious)
- Informative but not overwhelming
- Empowering the patient with actionable steps
- Use phrases like "You can expect..." and "To prepare..."
