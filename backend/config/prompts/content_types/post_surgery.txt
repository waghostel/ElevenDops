# Content Type: Post-Surgery Education

## Goal
Generate a POST-SURGERY patient education script that guides patients through their recovery process.

## Required Sections
1. **Opening** - Congratulations on completing the procedure
2. **Immediate Care** - First 24-48 hours instructions
3. **Wound Care** - How to care for the surgical site
4. **Pain Management** - Medication schedule and when to seek help
5. **Activity Restrictions** - What to avoid and gradual return to normal activities
6. **Warning Signs** - When to call the doctor or go to the emergency room
7. **Follow-up** - Appointment reminders and next steps
8. **Closing** - Encouragement for recovery journey

## Tone Guidelines
- Supportive and encouraging
- Clear and action-oriented
- Emphasize safety and when to seek help
- Use phrases like "During your recovery..." and "It is normal to..."
