# Content Type: Lifestyle Guidance

## Goal
Generate a lifestyle education script that helps patients understand daily habits and self-care practices to manage their condition.

## Required Sections
1. **Introduction** - Importance of lifestyle in managing the condition
2. **Diet** - Nutritional recommendations and foods to include/avoid
3. **Exercise** - Physical activity recommendations and restrictions
4. **Sleep** - Rest and sleep hygiene tips
5. **Stress Management** - Relaxation techniques and mental health
6. **Daily Routine** - Practical tips for integrating care into daily life
7. **Support** - Resources and support systems available
8. **Closing** - Encouragement and next steps

## Tone Guidelines
- Motivating and supportive
- Practical with actionable tips
- Non-judgmental and encouraging
- Use phrases like "Small changes can make a big difference..." and "Consider trying..."
