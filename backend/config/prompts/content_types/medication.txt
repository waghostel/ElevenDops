# Content Type: Medication Instructions

## Goal
Generate a medication education script that helps patients understand how to properly take their prescribed medications.

## Required Sections
1. **Introduction** - Overview of the medication and its purpose
2. **How to Take** - Dosage, timing, with/without food
3. **What to Expect** - Expected effects and timeline
4. **Side Effects** - Common side effects and what is normal
5. **Interactions** - Foods, drinks, or other medications to avoid
6. **Storage** - How to properly store the medication
7. **Missed Dose** - What to do if a dose is missed
8. **Warning Signs** - When to stop and contact the doctor

## Tone Guidelines
- Clear and precise
- Safety-focused
- Practical and actionable
- Use phrases like "Take this medication..." and "If you experience..."
